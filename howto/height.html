Height is a new mechanic to the D12 Only System. In a nutshell it adjusts damage and opponents difficulty to hit the target. As creatures get bigger, they will do more damage but become easier to hit. As creatures become smaller they do less damage but become harder to hit. Below is a usable chart on size categories:
