Below is a table of the available skills and their descriptions, and, except for block, may be rolled for whether the character has proficiency in the skill or not. Combat Skill proficiencies add +1 to the skill per rank, whereas other Skill Proficiences add +2 to the skill per rank. Characters will get a number of Skill Proficiency Rankss from their class, as well as choose a skill to increase the rank of at first level. Each level beyond first they will get another Skill Proficiency Rank to distribute.

Combat Skills
Skill Attribute Skill Description
Block Agility This is the character's ability to block attacks from hitting them.
Melee Strength This is the character's proficiency to wield Melee weapons.
Ranged Agility This is the character's proficiency to wield Ranged weapons.
General Skills
Skill Attribute Skill Description
Athletics Strength This is the character's athletic ability (i.e. running, swimming, or climbing)
Lockpicking Intellect This is the character's ability to disable locks and other similar devices.
Perception Intellect This is the character's awareness of the world around them.
Stealth Agility This is the character's ability to hide, move silently, or use sleight of hand.
Survival Endurance This is the character's ability to survive harsh conditions or scenarios away from civilization.
Social Skills
Skill Attribute Skill Description
Deception Charisma This is the character's ability to mislead, misdirect, or lie to another person.
Intimidation Charisma This is the character's ability to coerce others into listening to them through aggressive means.
Persuasion Charisma This is the character's altogether charm and ability to get others to agree with them.
Knowledge Skills
Skill Attribute Skill Description
Arcane Intellect This is the character's understanding of magic and how to cast it.
Crafting Intellect This is the character's understanding of creating objects from their base components.
Whether it be a work of art or a simple bench.
Cultures Intellect This is the character's understanding of different cultures around the nine realms and beyond.
Science Intellect This is the character's understanding of the fundamental laws of the universe and things in it.
Things like Math, Biology, Astronomy, and more.
Underworld Intellect This is the character's understanding of the criminal underworld and its inner workings.
