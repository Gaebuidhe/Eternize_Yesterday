These are particular abilities that each class typically teaches, but its on the player to pick for their character. Things like throwing weapons, modifying magitechnology, or even blocking for an ally are represented in the class abilities. At character creation each player will pick two of the starting abilities to further flesh out their character. Each level after they will pick an additional ability and at level five they will have the option to begin taking advanced abilities.
