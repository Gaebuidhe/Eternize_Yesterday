Character creation is meant to develop functioning character within the scope of the setting. Below outlines the basic process to creating a character in simple terms and instructions. It should also be noted, unless otherwise mentioned by the Game Master (GM), that stories focus on themes of exploration and unknowns of the universe at large. Will you play a Jotun Mercenary, an Alfar Scholar, or become the specialized Arcane Engineer?

It should be noted and understood that players are in a group environment. Players should not feel hampered in the character creation process, but your characters will be involved with a party for a marginal amount of time, hopefully. Respect one another above table, and have fun its just a game. If one character has issue with another, that should not leave the in character aspect and boil into out of character conversation.

Character Sheets or regular paper are suggested for use in recording your character.

1. Choose Race

There are a number of races that inhabit the Universe of Eternize Yesterday and playable characters will typically belong to one of these six. Use their attributes for the base of which your character is created.
Above it was mentioned that characters are typically of the following six races, if you have an idea for a race confer with your GM first.
⁍ Alfar
⁍ Aos sí
⁍ Dwarf
⁍ Human
⁍ Jotun
⁍ Leprechaun

2. Choose Class

A character's class represents his particular training or specializations. They give characters attributes and and skill proficiencies.
This is not an all inclusive list of potential class avenues. If you feel there needs to be another class, work with the GM to create one that fits your character motif
