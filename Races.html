Homebrewing Races

Some players or GM's may want to add to this list of races. Feel free to do so if you feel that there is something missing, but before you do there may be a few things to consider first. Is the race analogous to something that pre-exists? Would it be an offshoot of a pre-existing race? Would this race disbalance the game to one degree or another? Below are some instructions on setting up your new race.

1. Determine Attributes: distribute appropriately 13 points among the five attributes as they might commonly be represented by the race.
2. Determin Size: most races will be of medium size, however some races will be smaller or larger than the norm. This will affect certain aspects of play so consider this step carefully.
3. Determine Emergent Magic: All races have a type of emergent magical behavior. Whether it be something unique to the race or based on another race's behavior, this is the race's aptitude for certain types of magic.
4. Determine Background: This is race's story, their planet, their reason for existing. It does not necessarily have to be as indepth as the pre-existing races already created, but it should have some background to it to facilitate working them into the story.
